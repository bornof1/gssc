Load Monitoring

We track and we measure the physical demands placed on players during training and competition. The goal of load monitoring is to optimize player performance, reduce the risk of injury, and improve recovery time. We use several methods like GPS tracking, heart rate monitors, and motion analysis systems. Some of the key metrics that can be measured through load monitoring : Distance covere, High-intensity running, Accelerations and decelerations, Heart rate, Sleep and recovery...

Neural Cognition

Our objectives is to improve the player developement process by using cognitive training programs.These programs can include tasks and activities that target specific aspects of neural cognition, such as perception, attention, learning, memory, and decision-making. We use a lot of methods to quantify aspects of neural cognition in football players like : Reaction time tests, attentional control tasks, working memory tasks, neuropsychological tests, performance assessments, GPS and motion analysis system...

Injury Risk Factors

Injury is a common risk in football, because is a physically demanding sport that requires players to perform a variety of movements. That's why our teams will work with you to reduce the risk of injury and provide you with strategies to prioritize injury prevention by studying your previous injury history and their causes, treat fatigue, treat poor physical conditioning, study the improper technique that you use that increase chances of injury, provide you with the proper warm-up and cool-down routine before and after training and matches, study the surfaces that increase the risk of injury in your cases...

By analyzing event data, coaches and performance analysts can make informed decisions to improve tactical performance, identify areas for improvement, and optimize player selection. Additionally, event data can be used to compare player and team performance, as well as to inform tactical decisions during matches.
